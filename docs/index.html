Ilan Ehrlich

Welcome!

I am a mathematics master's student at ETH Zurich, currently doing my M2 internship. I work at Loria in Nancy, France, in the Caramba team under Jean Kieffer.

My project concerns modular polynomials and their improvement and generalization to higher dimension.

I am generally interested in algebraic geometry, number theory, and their applications to cryptography, especially isogeny-based. My focus is particularly on abelian varieties and moduli spaces.
